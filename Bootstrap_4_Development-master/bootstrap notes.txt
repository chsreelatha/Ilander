mobile first approach

padding & margin:-
-------------------
for margin left:- ml-0, ml-1, ml-2....ml-6
for margin right:- mr
for margin top:- mt
for margin bottom:- mb

ml+mr=mx
mt+mb=my

mx+my=m

for padding :- pl, pr, pt, pb, px, py
